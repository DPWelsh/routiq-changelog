## What this is

Most clinic management software has a dashboard. Most of those dashboards are an afterthought. We're building one that's the front door — the screen a clinic owner opens with their morning coffee.

It should answer the questions owners actually ask:

- How many appointments today, and is anyone at risk of no-showing?
- How much revenue did Routiq's AI handle this week — bookings, deposits, follow-ups?
- Who's lapsed and would respond to outreach?
- What did the AI say to my patients yesterday — anything weird?
- What's outstanding, and how long has it been outstanding?

## Why it pairs with the other roadmap items

The payments portal generates invoice and payment data. The multi-PMS parity work standardises the underlying tables. The eval harness ensures the AI activity logs are trustworthy. The dashboard is the surface that pulls all of it into one daily view.

We won't ship a dashboard until the data behind it is rock-solid. Right now, that's not all the way there. Soon.
